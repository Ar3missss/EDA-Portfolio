# EDA Portfolio

**Aryan Pathania (Ar3missss)**

A collection of Exploratory Data Analysis projects done using Python, Pandas and Matplotlib. Each notebook covers a different real-world dataset with data cleaning, feature extraction and visual analysis.

---

## Projects

### 1. Netflix Content Analysis
**File:** `netflix-analysis.ipynb`

Analysed the Netflix titles dataset to understand how content is distributed across types, countries, genres and time periods.

**Questions Explored:**
- Are movies more common than TV shows?
- Which countries produce the most content on Netflix?
- What are the most frequent genres?
- How has Netflix content grown over time?
- What type of audience does Netflix target based on ratings?
- What is the average duration of movies?
- Which genres are popular in specific countries?

**Dataset:** [Netflix Shows - Kaggle]*https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/netflix-shows*

---

### 2. Sales Data Analysis
**File:** `sales-analysis.ipynb`

Analysed a sales dataset covering transactions across regions, product categories, sales reps and payment methods.

**Questions Explored:**
- Which region generates the most sales?
- Who are the top performing sales representatives?
- Which product category contributes the most revenue?
- How have sales trended monthly and quarterly?
- What is the most common payment method?
- How do Online and Retail channels compare?
- What is the distribution of new vs returning customers?
- What is the average discount across product categories?

**Dataset:** *https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vinothkannaece/sales-dataset*

---

### 3. Zomato Restaurant Analysis
**File:** `zomato-analysis.ipynb`

Analysed the Zomato restaurant dataset to explore restaurant distribution, popular cuisines, pricing and ratings.

**Questions Explored:**
- Which cities have the most restaurants listed on Zomato?
- What are the most popular cuisines?
- How are restaurants distributed across price ranges?
- How many restaurants offer online delivery and table booking?
- What is the distribution of restaurant ratings?
- Which price range gets the highest average rating?
- Which cuisines have the highest average ratings?

**Dataset:** *https://github.com/SouGuit/Zomato_Dataset_Analysis*
